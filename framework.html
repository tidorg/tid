Strategic Framework & Partners

TID works with strategic partners and donors to implement sustainable programs across education, health, livelihoods, and protection.

Our Strategic Framework

Education & Skills

Enhancing access to education, vocational training, and lifelong learning for communities.

Health & Nutrition

Improving healthcare access, promoting nutrition, and building resilient health systems.

Agriculture & Livelihood

Supporting sustainable livelihoods, food security, and income-generating activities.

Protection & Advocacy

Ensuring human rights, protection of vulnerable groups, and community advocacy.

Integration & Community Development

Promoting inclusive communities, social cohesion, and participatory development.

Our Partners & Donors

TID

Empowering communities through integrated development strategies.
