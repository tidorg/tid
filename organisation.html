Organisation

TID (Tintilin Initiative for Development) is dedicated to transparent governance and effective program management. Our structure ensures accountability and collaboration.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower communities through education, health, livelihood, and protection initiatives. Our vision is a self-reliant and thriving community.

Organisational Structure

Founder & Board Chair

  • Established TID with vision and purpose.
  • Provides overall strategic guidance.
  • Represents TID in key partnerships.

Executive Director

  • Leads daily operations and programs.
  • Implements policies and strategic plans.
  • Oversees staff, volunteers, and initiatives.

Secretary General

  • Maintains official records and communications.
  • Prepares meeting agendas and minutes.
  • Supports board and executive team.
