About Us

ICDI is a humanitarian and development organization committed to empowering vulnerable communities through integrated, sustainable solutions.

Our Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

To create empowered, self-reliant communities thriving in sustainable development.

Our Mission

To provide holistic and integrated solutions addressing social, economic, and environmental needs of vulnerable communities.

Our Values

  • Integrity
  • Accountability
  • Inclusiveness
  • Collaboration
  • Sustainability

Digital Skills & Education

Empowering youth through digital literacy, ICT training, and educational support.

Health & Nutrition

Promoting access to essential healthcare and community health programs.

Child Protection & Advocacy

Strengthening child protection systems and promoting rights at community level.

Livelihoods & Economic Empowerment

Supporting income-generating activities and vocational skills for community resilience.

Peacebuilding & Social Cohesion

Facilitating dialogue, conflict resolution, and community-based initiatives to promote unity.

ICDI

Empowering communities through integrated development strategies.
